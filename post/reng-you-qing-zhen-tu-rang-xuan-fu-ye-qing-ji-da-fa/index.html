仍有「清真土壤悬浮液清洁大法」

2020-12-14

感谢@garlicjuice 先生的分享。
这是2014年的马来西亚第三版Halal认证流程工作手册（manual procedure for malaysia halal certification 3rd revision），里面仍有「清真土壤悬浮液清洁大法」的条文。

2.5  Sertu (Ritual Cleansing)
  Sertu means cleansing something from mughallazah najs such as dogs, 
pigs and their descendents. Mughallazah najs should be cleansed by using 
one time water mixed with soil and followed with six times clean/ mutlak 
water.
----------------MANUAL PROCEDURE FOR
MALAYSIA HALAL CERTIFICATION (THIRD REVISION) 2014
